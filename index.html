About DustDeep

Brooklyn-born, London-based, with roots in Berlin. Dustdeep is a collective exploring the spaces between electronic, percussive, global bass and melodic sounds.

We study artists’ previous works to understand their sonic identity, then collaborate to craft music that feels like a natural extension of their world.

Each release is a dialogue between the creator, the crowd and the room it’s meant for.
