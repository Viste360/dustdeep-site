<section class="section" id="about">

  <h2>About DustDeep</h2>


  <p>Brooklyn-born, London-based, with roots in Berlin. Dustdeep is a collective of producers, engineers and writers exploring the spaces between electronic, percussive, global bass and melodic sounds.</p>


  <p>We study artists’ previous works to understand their sonic identity, then collaborate to craft music that feels like a natural extension of their world.</p>


  <p>Each release is a dialogue between the creator, the crowd and the room it’s meant for. Dustdeep Records: shaping identity through sound.</p>

</section>
